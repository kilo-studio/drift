# Name and positioning

Decide the public name and App Store positioning. Avoid "vape" in the name to keep App Store options open. Position as harm reduction / mindfulness, not "smoke this less."

## Resolution

**Name: Drift.** Verb (cloud drift, drifting between hits) and noun (a slow change, a mood). Soft, abstract, fits the Ghibli aesthetic.

**Tagline candidates** (pick one for App Store):
- *Notice the gaps.*
- *A small, gentle space between hits.*
- *Free for X — and counting.*

**App Store category**: Health & Fitness or Lifestyle. Lifestyle reads less medicalized.

## Notes

The hero framing is "free for X" which is already perfect harm-reduction language. Don't ever say "quit." Don't show streaks that reset on failure.

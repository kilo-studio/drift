drift

a small, gentle space between hits

Drift is a quiet iOS app that helps you notice the time between hits — without streaks, shame, or nags.

A cloud spirit watches the gaps. Its eyes get bigger and the sky fills with sparkles the longer you've drifted. Log a hit and the field gently resets. No badges to earn, no fire emoji counting up — just a present-tense, neutral view of right now.

Your data lives on your device. Nothing leaves it.

what's inside

  • The spirit. A cloud character whose size and sparkle field track the ratio between your current gap and your rolling average.
  • The dashboard. Today's sessions, average gap, longest stretches, when cravings tend to hit, and a 30-day rolling average of how the gaps are stretching.
  • Logging. Tap the + tab, bind the Action Button to "Log a hit in Drift," or use a Lock Screen shortcut. Notifications are scheduled locally and you can pick which kinds you want.
  • History. A month calendar with donut day cells and per-day stats. Edit or delete any hit.
  • Export. Settings → Data → "export hits" writes every logged hit to a JSON file. Save it anywhere — Files, iCloud Drive, email it to yourself.

privacy

Drift declares "Data Not Collected" on the App Store, and that's accurate. No analytics, no third-party SDKs, no servers we run. Everything lives on your device.
